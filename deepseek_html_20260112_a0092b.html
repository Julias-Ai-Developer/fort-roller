ABOUT FORT ROLLERS

We are a community-driven skating company focused on empowering skaters of all levels through quality gear, professional training, and unforgettable events.

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2015 by professional skaters Alex "Grind" Morgan and Jamie "Flip" Chen, Fort Rollers started as a small skate crew and grew into a leading urban sports company. Our mission is to make skating accessible, safe, and exciting for everyone.

Skate Culture First

We celebrate the creativity and freedom of skate culture in everything we do.

Community Driven

Our skaters are at the heart of every decision, event, and product we create.

Quality & Safety

We provide only the highest quality gear that meets strict safety standards.
