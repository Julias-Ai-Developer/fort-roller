About Edge Skating Club

Founded in 2018, Edge Skating Club has become Fort Portal's premier destination for roller skating training and community. Our mission is to make skating accessible to everyone, from complete beginners to competitive athletes.

We offer professional coaching, premium equipment, and a supportive environment where skaters of all ages and skill levels can thrive. Our experienced coaches bring years of competitive experience and a passion for teaching.

Our Programs

Kids Program

Fun and safe skating lessons for children aged 4-12. Our specialized kids coaches focus on building confidence, basic skills, and a love for skating.

Beginner Training

Start your skating journey with our comprehensive beginner program. Learn proper techniques, balance, and basic skating skills.

Competitive Training

Advanced training for competitive skaters. Improve speed, agility, and technical skills with our championship-winning coaches.

Group Sessions

Join our group training sessions for social skating. Perfect for maintaining skills, meeting fellow skaters, and having fun.

Private Coaching

One-on-one coaching tailored to your specific goals. Whether you're preparing for competition or just want to improve, we customize your training.

Weekend Workshops

Specialized workshops on technique, fitness, and competition preparation. Join us for intensive training sessions and skill development.
