How We Coach Skaters

Safety First

Every session starts with protection checks, warmups, and controlled drills to build confidence safely.

Progressive Learning

We break down skills into clear levels so each skater improves with structure and measurable milestones.

Performance Training

Advanced athletes train speed, balance, and race tactics with targeted routines and feedback.

Community Support

Our culture keeps skaters motivated through peer support, events, and coach mentorship.

Athlete Development Pathway

1

Starter Level

4 to 6 weeks focused on stance, stopping, turning, and skating balance basics.

2

Skill Builder

8 to 12 weeks of edge control, endurance laps, and controlled crossover techniques.

3

Competitive Track

Race starts, pace strategy, sprint intervals, and event preparation for club competitions.
